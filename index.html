Each property within Castle Pines Metro District has been analyzed in a three step process to create an accurate calculation of irrigation areas. This includes programmatic analysis of aerial imagery utilizing machine learning, visual examination of aerial imagery, and on site field verification. This geographic analysis was done to assist the metro district's irrigation rebate program.
